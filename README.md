# My Favorite Movie: Moana

![Moana Movie Poster](https://www.themoviedb.org/t/p/original/6iHQpFiTg0QbKYac5Mprhx7tXo3.jpg)

**Moana** is a Disney animated film about a courageous Polynesian girl who sets sail across the ocean to restore the heart of Te Fiti and save her island, guided by her ancestors and accompanied by the demigod Maui.

## Why I Love Moana
- Inspiring story about **self-discovery** and **courage**
- Beautiful animation and visuals
- Amazing soundtrack by Lin-Manuel Miranda 
- Strong female lead who doesn’t rely on a prince

## Favorite Song
> *"How Far I'll Go"*  
> *“If the wind in my sail on the sea stays behind me, one day I'll know. If I go, there's just no telling how far I'll go.”*

## Memorable Characters
- **Moana** – Brave and adventurous heroine
- **Maui** – Demigod with a magical fish hook
- **Heihei** – The hilarious chicken sidekick 
- **Tamatoa** - The shiny, treasure-hoarding giant crab who loves glam
